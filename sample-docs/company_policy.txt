COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT

Effective Date: January 2025

1. WORKPLACE CONDUCT

All employees are expected to maintain professional behavior at all times. This includes:
- Respectful communication with colleagues and clients
- Adherence to company dress code
- Punctuality for meetings and work hours
- Confidentiality regarding company information

2. REMOTE WORK POLICY

Remote work is permitted for eligible employees with manager approval. Requirements include:
- Stable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps)
- Dedicated workspace free from distractions
- Availability during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM EST)
- Regular check-ins with team members

3. VACATION AND TIME OFF

Employees accrue vacation time at a rate of 1.25 days per month. Time off requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal. Blackout dates apply during peak business periods.

4. DATA SECURITY

All employees must:
- Use strong passwords (minimum 12 characters)
- Enable two-factor authentication on all company accounts
- Never share credentials with others
- Report security incidents immediately to IT security team

5. PERFORMANCE REVIEWS

Annual performance reviews are conducted in Q4. Employees will receive feedback on:
- Goal achievement
- Technical skills development
- Collaboration and teamwork
- Areas for improvement

For questions about company policies, contact HR at hr@company.com.
